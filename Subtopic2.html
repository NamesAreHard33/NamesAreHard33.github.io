 What is an AI Art Generator?
Site Author
 Madeline Clarke
Abstract


 This article covers the uses of artificial intelligence in art and how it is used in the world today as well as the controversies and benefits of using AI art generators. It discusses realistic images that could pass as photos as well as more creative and artistic looking images with vibrant colors and unrealistic physics. The article looks at the real-world applications for these programs as businesses use AI generated images and videos for marketing and advertising. The article stays objective, looking at the benefits of AI art like speed, accessibility, and inspiration but still realizing the drawbacks like legal issues, moral ethics, and ingrained biases.

What AI Art Means for Society
Site Author
 Kayla Yup
Abstract
 This article talks about how people without the skill of an artist can create art using AI art generators. It talks about AI art as a tool for the public, not a replacement for artists. AI art is easier to create than human art, however, the article shows that some argue that the creativity of art is still intact as humans are prompting the program and coming up with the ideas for the art as well as skillfully creating the AI programs themself. A study showed that Yale undergrads could only tell the difference between AI art and human art slightly more than 54% of the time.
