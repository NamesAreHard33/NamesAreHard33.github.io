 Timeline of AI Art
Site Author
 Marnie Benney and Pete Kistler
Abstract


 This article gives an in-depth timeline of machines and artificial intelligence’s development in art. The timeline spans from 1763 to 2019, although it was updated in 2020. It covers many programs, milestones, and advancements. This article delves into music as well as images, referencing Taryn Southern, a pop artist that uses AI in collaboration with human efforts to create music. The article also discusses other groundbreaking programs such as the Painting Fool, a program by Simon Colton, and Google’s Deep Dream; all extremely relevant developments. The article mentions dancing robots, more specifically the NAO bot, which is just another way this technology is being applied to art.

How Did A.I. Art Evolve?
Site Author
 Naomi Rea
Abstract
 This article gives a timeline of the development of technology into artificial intelligence and how it was applied to art in both ancient history as well as in our modern society. It briefly covers present day technology used in AI image generators and chat bots but focuses more on the key movements that gave us that technology. It also covers the milestones of AI art such as an AI generated piece, Portrait de Edmond de Belamy, that sold for $432,000 at an art auction. Not only does this article take a deep dive into the history of AI art, but it also asks important questions along the way about the moral and ethical aspects of these programs as well as the possible biases that have been found.
